    Upcoming Events

  • Veterans Day program

    The annual Veterans Day program at Madison happens at 1:00p.m. on Friday, November 9 in the Madison auditorium. Every year, the Madsion Band works with our choir, and various other clubs and organizations within the school, to honor those who have served in our Armed Forces. All veterans and parents are invited to this free event, and veterans are encouraged to attend and be recognized. Please come and join us for this wonderful event honoring our veterans.

  • Winter Concert

    This year, the Winter Concert for Madison's band and choir will be at 6:30p.m. on Thursday, December 13. As always, this years concert will be a collaboration between both the choir and the band. This concert is free, so please join us to support two of our great fine arts programs.

  • JSYO Side-by-Side and Christmas Concert

    The Jackson Symphony Youth Orchestra (JSYO) is a youth organization run by the Jackson Symphony, where student try out and get a chance to play with the Jackson Symphony, as well as with other players their age. This year, we have 7 participants from Madison in the JSYO. The Side-by-side concert with the Jackson Symphony is Saturday, December 1st at 7:30p.m. at the Carl Perkins Civic Center. The following night, on Sunday, December 2nd is the JSYO solo concert at 3:00p.m. in the Jackson General Hospital Atrium. Please come to support our many great students from Madison at these 2 events.

    Recent Events

  • Talent Show

    Once again, the Madison Academic Talent Show was a huge success. There were many acts, including singing and dancing. This event not only was a lot of fun for the students, but it was a great fundraiser for the band. Please congratulate any students who participated in this event, and thank you to everyone who helped the band in this amazing event.

  • WTSBOA Concert Festival in Jackson and State Concert Festival

    This year, like always, the Madison band participated in the Jackson Concert Festival hosted at Liberty High School. For the 2017-2018 school year, however, the band had a perfect score of 1 out of 5 in every single category. Furthermore, as their score from the previous year was almost perfect as well, the band was invited to play at the State Concert Festival at Austin Peay State University. This is a huge achievement, so please congratulate these students for their hard work.

  • All West

    The All West band is an honor band that encompasses all of west Tennessee. Students must first audition for this event, and if accepted, work on their given music. Then all of the students in West Tennessee will meet together at the All West Clinic for the first time, rehearse, and perform. this past year, Madison had 6 representatives at the All West Clinic. The Madison Band is very proud of these students.
