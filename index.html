Upcoming Events

  • Veterans Day program

    The annual Veterans Day program at Madison happens at 1:00p.m. on Friday,
    November 9 in the Madison auditorium. Every year, the Madsion Band works
    with our choir, and various other clubs and organizations within the school, to
    honor those who have served in our Armed Forces. All veterans and parents are
    invited to this free event, and veterans are encouraged to attend and be recognized.
    Please come and join us for this wonderful event honoring our veterans.

  • Winter Concert

    This year, the Winter Concert for Madison's band and choir will be at 6:30p.m. on
    Thursday, December 13. As always, this years concert will be a collaboration between
    both the choir and the band. This concert is free, so please join us to support two of
    our great fine arts programs.

  • JSYO Side-by-Side and Christmas Concert

    The Jackson Symphony Youth Orchestra (JSYO) is a youth organization run by the Jackson
    Symphony, where student try out and get a chance to play with the Jackson Symphony,
    as well as with other players their age. This year, we have 7 participants from Madison in the
    JSYO. The Side-by-side concert with the Jackson Symphony is Saturday, December 1st at 7:30p.m.
    at the Carl Perkins Civic Center. The following night, on Sunday, December 2nd is the JSYO solo
    concert at 3:00p.m. in the Jackson General Hospital Atrium. Please come to support our many great
    students from Madison at these 2 events.
